1. HP Victus amd varient

This variant is ideal for users who prefer AMD's architecture and want a laptop that can handle demanding applications with ease.

Key Features:

  • AMD Ryzen 7 7435HS processor

  • 16GB DDR4 RAM
  • Customizable RGB lighting

Why I like this varient ?

I like the HP Victus amd variant because it offers a great balance between performance and design.
